  • Shipping & Handling

    Shipping rates are calculated based on the value of the order subtotal. Shipping rates vary based on the shipping destination. Click here for more information on our shipping rates.

    * Certain oversize items will incur an additional fee and will be noted on the product detail.

    * Priority and Express shipping will incur an additional fee.

    * Items purchased in multiple quantities may incur additional fees.

    Many larger items ship by Truck delivery. The freight company will contact you in advance to schedule a delivery date and time. Someone must be present at the time of delivery and a signature will be required. Please note that the drivers are contracted to deliver to the end of the truck only. You will be responsible for unloading the truck. Drivers do not provide inside delivery (over the threshold) as part of their service. For this reason, especially for oversized items, we recommend that you have help available to unload and move the item to its final location.

    Free Shipping

    Any Free shipping promotions are only applicable for delivery within the contiguous 48 United States.

    Local Pick up

    If you live within 100 miles of our Little Tikes facility in Hudson, OH you can choose local pick during check out for your order. You will receive an email confirmation once your order is ready to be picked up.

    This process generally takes 2-3 buisness days. Orders are not processed on weekends or holidays.

    Price matching Policy

    Littletikes.com does not match prices with any of our online competitors or retail partners.

  • Cancellation Policy

    Registered users on Littletikes.com can cancel their order within one hour from placing the order. Once this window has expired, orders cannot be cancelled. We must process orders quickly in order to ensure a timely delivery. We apologize in advance that your order cannot be cancelled after the one hour window.

    Registered users can log in to the My AccountMy Account page to cancel their order. Orders placed by Guest checkout cannot be cancelled.

    You will receive an email confirmation that your order has been cancelled. Please note that there is a hold on the funds associated with the order that will drop off after the cancellation. Some credit card companies may hold the funds for up to 5 business days.
